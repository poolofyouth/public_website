About

Pool of Youth

We are a team of three Year 13 high school students from Marist College, New Zealand. Mr Paterson, our Digital Technology teacher, introduced us to the world of blockchain and has been guiding us through various exciting projects—including Pool of Youth!

Us students are interested in how we can leverage the Cardano blockchain in order to spark positive change. Pool of Youth is our attempt to do just that—providing a platform for young people to share and inspire in a digital world which we believe lacks significant youth input.
