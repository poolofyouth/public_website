Our Vision

Founded by three high school students from New Zealand, Pool of Youth is a collective focused on promoting young people in Cardano. We aim to elevate Cardano projects developed by like-minded students by providing a space for individuals to flaunt their work and profile. Through this, we hope to allow youth to connect with others, developing a Cardano network for young people across the world, and inspiring the changemakers, innovators and visionaries of the future.
